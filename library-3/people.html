The People

Reference Section - Taft Extension (RS-Taft Extension)

The staff at the RS-Taft Extension were incredibly welcoming and kind to us, which lightened the workload. Ms. Marvs was especially helpful, guiding me and readily sharing her knowledge when I was assigned to them. The library clerks were also easy to get along with and kind, and they taught me about various relevant services.

Reference Section - Design and Arts Benilde (RS-DAC)

The staff at RS-DAC were equally kind and knowledgeable. Sir Abe was notably hands-on with interns, offering step-by-step guidance and readily sharing his expertise.

Technical Section - Design and Arts College (TS-DAC)

The staff at TS-DAC was welcoming and kind, providing continuous guidance throughout my time. I gained significant knowledge in cataloging, acquisition, and other Technical Section processes there.
