About the NCCA

The National Commission for Culture and the Arts (NCCA), Philippines is the overall policy making body, coordinating, and grants giving agency for the preservation, development, and promotion of Philippine arts and culture; an executing agency for the policies it formulates; and tasked with administering the National Endowment Fund for Culture and the Arts (NEFCA) — a fund exclusively for the implementation of culture and arts programs and projects.

Additionally, the NCCA’s Special Library collections are focused on art, culture, and related fields, to meet the information needs of its members and staff, and to promote the preservation and study of cultural heritage.
