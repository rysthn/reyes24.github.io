The People

The people that I have worked with during my internship were all incredibly kind, approachable, smart, and responsible. People that I have worked with in the library were the librarian, Ma'am Jelome Domingo, and Supervisor Ma'am Delia Peregrina. They are both very supportive of us interns and always rooting for us without pressure. They consistently enquire and monitor our progress to determine whether the tasks they assign are challenging, allowing them to modify them accordingly. They are making sure that we're okay and not invalidating our ideas and our own perspective.

One thing that I also like or love about them is that they consider us interns like a librarian; I literally feel that they cared for and loved us. While in the records management section, we worked with the head, Ma'am Jeyd De Guzman, along with her staff, Ma'am Suzanne, Nemi, Sir Bert, Sir Roel, Sir Hernani, and Sir Manny. Same with my experience in the library section; they are all kind with the interns and consider us like their co-workers.
