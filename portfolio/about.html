About Me

Hello everyone! My name is Cindy D. Reyes. I'm 22 years old and from Navotas City. I'm currently a graduating student at the Philippine Normal University (PNU) Manila. I've also just completed internships at four types of libraries: the NCCA Special Library, the Emilio Aguinaldo College Library, the De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde Academic Library, and the National Library of the Philippines.

With that, let me share a bit about myself. As PNUans, we often face high expectations. At first, I thought I wouldn't be able to continue my studies here because I knew my weaknesses — a lack of confidence, social anxiety, and fear of public speaking — would be very challenging at PNU. I was overwhelmed and stressed, genuinely believing I couldn't handle it. For about four years, I've strived to become a better version of myself. I've tried to keep up with my classmates and have learned to love my course, even though it wasn't my dream course.

I'm still searching for what I want, for the right path for me, and for where I truly belong. I still feel somewhat lost. There are times when I ask myself, "Can I do this? Will I be able to handle it, or should I just let myself go with the flow?" I feel pressured because many people know what they want in life and what they're good at. But me? I still can't figure out what I want, and I'm hoping to find my own path someday.

There have been many failures that made me want to quit, and perhaps there are many more I will face. Despite that, I'm here. I survived! I'm somewhat proud of myself for making it this far. A future librarian! Maybe God has a purpose for all of this, and perhaps being a librarian is my calling, not just for myself, but also for the bayan, bata, and aklatan.

Through this portfolio, I will demonstrate how genuinely invested I am in my internships and how much I value this experience. It will also highlight my experiences, such as the challenges I faced, the lessons I learned, and the importance of librarianship that I've come to understand during my time as an intern.
