My Internship Experience

My experiences here were great. I adapted to the environment by learning how to interact effectively with different people. My interactions with librarians, clerks, and students were good, and I learned a lot from them. From helping students with their needs to understanding various library services, I saw that Benilde truly values information and caters to its students' needs.

On the other hand, I had an unexpected situation with one librarian that I still don't fully understand. That made me realize that not everyone in my environment is always "okay," and it taught me to be more aware of how I interact with others in the future. Despite this, it doesn't take away from the many positive experiences and valuable lessons I gained during my internship at Benilde.
